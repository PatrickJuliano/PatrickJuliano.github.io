Jamie

Jamie's reputation as a prodigy precedes her, much to her chagrin. Often touted as the brains and the brawn of the family, she struggles with the lofty expectations imposed upon her. Underworld Army officials have long eyed Jamie as a future general—though she'd much rather be a veterinarian.

Lily

The youngest among them, Lily is regarded by her sisters as an unpredictable force of chaos. She possesses the mysterious ability to pull seemingly anything from her pocket, one that she cannot yet control. Lily is slated to either usher in a new era for the Underworld, or bring it to its knees—whichever comes first.

Aria

Despite her status as next in line, Aria receives far less scrutiny than her younger sisters. She shows little interest in the position, for that matter; she prefers to concern herself with the Underworld's finer arts. Aria is distant toward her family for reasons that remain unclear.
